About Triviaplaza

Triviaplaza is made and maintained by Peter Meindertsma.
He created this site in 2001 and after several revisions, it currently has more than 120.000 questions in over 4.000 different quizzes.

Other quiz sites by Peter Meindertsma:

  • Dominoquiz.com - Prevent domino tiles from falling in this general knowledge quiz.
  • Popkwiz.com - Pop music quizzes about the 50s, 60s, 70s, 80s, 90s, 2000s and 2010s.
  • Actionquiz.com - Quiz show where human players compete with computer opponents.
